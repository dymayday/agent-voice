agent-voice: calm local companion

Visual identity explorations for a native macOS wrapper around a Bun/TypeScript daemon that turns completed coding-agent turns into one-line local Kokoro speech.

1. The Whisper Capsule

A rounded terminal/speech capsule: one command line becomes one spoken line. Feels Mac-native, private, and daemon-like without robot/speaker clichés.

2. Local Hearth

A private “listening hearth”: sound as warmth and presence, not volume. Good for a companion app that sits quietly until useful.

calm companionprivacy-first

3. The Margin Voice

A code note in the margin emitting a small acoustic contour. More editorial and summary-focused; best if app emphasizes readable history.
